***Alarm.*** Whenever a creature other than its creator comes within 60
feet of the golem, it releases an audible alarm sound which can
be heard out to a range of 300 feet.

***Immutable Form.*** The golem is immune to any spell or effect that
would alter its form.

***Magic Resistance.*** The golem has advantage on saving throws against
spells and other magical effects.

***Magic Weapon.*** The golem’s weapon attacks are magical.

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** The wood golem makes two slam attacks.

***Slam.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12
(2d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage.
