### Actions

***Battleaxe.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d8 + 1) slashing damage.

***Frost Breath.*** The frost dwarf exhales a 15-foot cone of freezing air.
Each creature in the area must make a DC 12 Constitution saving throw,
taking 7 (2d6) cold damage on a failed saving throw, or half as much
damage on a successful one.
